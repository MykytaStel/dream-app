# dream-capture.md

## Feature
Dream Capture

## Goal
Make dream recording fast, calm, and reliable in the first moments after waking up.

## Why it matters
This is the most important product moment in the app. If capture feels slow or noisy, the whole product weakens.

## Core UX goals
- open fast
- minimal friction
- support text and voice
- draft safety
- no clutter
- premium feel
