# MONETIZATION.md

## Monetization Goal

Monetization should support the product without damaging trust.

The app deals with personal emotional content, so monetization must feel:
- respectful
- optional
- premium
- fair
- transparent

---

## Recommended Model

Use a **freemium** model.

### Free Tier Should Include
- core dream journaling
- editing and deleting entries
- tags and moods
- timeline/journal access
- basic search and filters
- reminders
- local storage
- basic privacy settings

The free version must feel genuinely useful.

---

## Premium Tier Can Include

### Strong Candidates
- cloud sync
- cross-device backup
- premium themes
- advanced insights
- premium widgets
- advanced exports
- richer history filtering
- AI summaries
- AI symbol extraction
- dream boards / visual cards

### Optional Nice-to-Haves
- expanded customization
- curated symbol packs
- deeper trend reports

---

## What Not to Lock

Avoid paywalling:
- basic journaling
- basic trust/privacy
- core reading of existing entries
- simple reminders
- basic export if needed for trust

Users should never feel trapped inside their own personal data.

---

## Monetization Rollout Strategy

### Stage 1
No paywall at launch or use an extremely light premium tease.
Focus on retention and product love first.

### Stage 2
Introduce premium after users clearly understand product value.

### Stage 3
Add deeper premium value only once advanced features truly exist.

---

## Pricing Direction

Possible subscription positioning:
- monthly premium for flexibility
- annual premium as best-value option
- occasional lifetime launch offer if desired

The product should present premium as:
- deeper insight
- more personalization
- stronger backup and portability
- richer reflection tools

Not as a forced unlock of basic dignity.

---

## Premium Messaging Principles

Good messaging:
- calm
- elegant
- benefit-focused
- non-pushy

Bad messaging:
- manipulative urgency
- guilt-based retention
- “unlock your hidden destiny” style claims
- exaggerated mystical promises

---

## Best Revenue Drivers

Most realistic premium drivers:
1. sync and backup
2. advanced insights
3. personalization and themes
4. AI-assisted features
5. export quality and archive tools

---

## Monetization Summary

The best monetization path is to:
- earn trust first
- keep the free version strong
- charge for depth, convenience, and premium polish
- never make users feel exploited inside a personal reflection app
